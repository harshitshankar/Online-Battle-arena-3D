Privacy Policy for Battle Arena 3D

Last updated: [TODAY'S DATE]

This Privacy Policy describes how Battle Arena 3D ("we", "our", or "app") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our multiplayer game.

1. Information We Collect

Our app collects only the minimum information needed to function:

  • Username: The name you enter when joining a game (only visible to other players in your room).
  • Room Code: The code you enter to join multiplayer rooms.
  • Microphone Access: Used only if you enable Voice Chat. Audio is streamed peer-to-peer between players and is NOT recorded, stored, or shared with us or third parties.
  • Game Data: Position, health, score, and similar game-related info shared in real time with other players in your room.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, address, phone).
  • We do NOT track your location.
  • We do NOT save chat messages or voice recordings.
  • We do NOT use cookies or analytics trackers.
  • We do NOT show ads or sell your data.

3. How We Use Information

All collected info is used solely to enable real-time multiplayer gameplay. Game data is held in server memory only while you're connected and is deleted when you disconnect.

4. Voice Chat

Voice chat uses WebRTC peer-to-peer technology. Voice data is transmitted directly between players' devices and does NOT pass through or get stored on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google STUN servers (stun.l.google.com) to help establish peer-to-peer voice connections. No personal data is shared with Google through this.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used our app, please contact us to remove their session data.

7. Data Security

All connections are encrypted using HTTPS. Game session data is automatically deleted when the room becomes empty.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't store any personal data, there's nothing to delete or export. Simply disconnecting from a game removes all your session info.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
